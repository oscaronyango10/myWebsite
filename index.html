About Me

Hello, my name is Oscar Onyango. I am a seasoned professional with a strong foundation in Information System Security and Electrical and Electronics Engineering, complemented by extensive experience in project management, research, and product development.

I have led the design and deployment of various solutions addressing critical challenges in utility management, combining technical ingenuity with strategic vision. My project management skills have been honed through leading and contributing to impactful initiatives such as:

  • The International Telecommunication Union (ITU)’s Connect2Recover
  • Disaster Prediction and Management (DPM)
  • Dynamic Spectrum Alliance (DSA)’s Framework for TV White Space (TVWS) in Kenya
  • Spectrum Sharing in 5G/6G Networks

These roles have strengthened my technical expertise and enhanced my ability to coordinate multi-disciplinary teams, manage resources effectively, and deliver results within tight timelines.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and create sustainable impact in underserved communities. My expertise in strategic planning, data-driven decision-making, stakeholder engagement, and large-scale project execution has positioned me to lead transformative initiatives aligning technology with social and economic development.
